Services of repair of technic

What are we doing?

  • repair telephons;
  • repair TV's;
  • repair personal computers (PC);
  • repair cars etc.

Complex of work carried out:

  • equipment maintenance;
  • scheduled equipment repair;
  • accumulation and study of operating and repair experience, establishing the optimal frequency and duration of major, medium and current repairs;
  • the use of modern diagnostic tools for monitoring and predicting the technical condition of equipment and making a decision on the need for repair;
  • introduction of progressive forms of organization and management of repairs using computers and information technologies;
  • widespread introduction of specialization of repair work;
  • quality control of work performed during the repair process and quality control of repaired equipment;
  • timely provision of repair work with materials, spare parts and accessories;
  • analysis of the parameters of the technical condition of the equipment before and after repair according to the test results.

Maintenance operations can be carried out on working or stopped equipment, while the scope of work in a generalized form is as follows:

  • bypassing according to the schedule and technical inspection of the operating equipment to control its technical condition and timely detection of defects;
  • control of the technical condition of equipment using external means of control or diagnostics, including control of portable equipment for tightness, vibration, etc., visual and measuring control of individual assembly units of equipment with partial, if necessary, its disassembly;
  • replacement of sight glasses, loading of shot and balls, inspection and replacement of defective hammer mill hammers, cleaning of oil, fuel oil, air and water filters and sedimentation tanks, cleaning of water treatment plant grids, tube plates of condensers and oil coolers;
  • inspection and testing of control mechanisms, bearings, valve drives, tightening of oil seals, adjustment of blowing, shot blasting, gas and pneumatic impulse, ultrasonic and electric impulse devices;
  • purging of heating surfaces, elimination of slags, suction cups, dust, vapors, leaks of water, oil, gas and fuel oil, maintenance of water meters;
  • cleaning lubricating fluids with external cleaning devices or changing the lubricant (lubricants, oils etc.);
  • control of the serviceability of measuring systems and measuring instruments, including their calibration;
  • observation of supports, fasteners, indicators of the position of pipelines;
  • check for the serviceability of equipment, carried out with the removal of equipment from work or on working equipment;
  • elimination of individual defects identified as a result of condition monitoring, serviceability checks;
  • inspection and testing of equipment when it is in reserve or on storage, in order to identify and eliminate deviations from the normal state.
